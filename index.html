About us

Our Tradition Of Best Hospitality

Hill on misty house is lot more than just another homestay. It is a perfect holiday home where you can enjoy the serene beauty as well as the hospitality that makes you feel at home. This ancestral home belongs to Sindhu who maintains it with at most care. Hill on misty house is very unique in its architecture. its cooling mud walls, clay tiled floor, spacious rooms opening to the breath taking view of muvattupuzha river leaves you mesmerized. The house is in the midst of coconut tree, mango tree, nutmeg and a wide variety of birds call this home .
